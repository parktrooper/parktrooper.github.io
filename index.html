About ParkTrooper

Built on Service.
Committed to Austin.

ParkTrooper LLC was founded by Najee Lecque, a U.S. military veteran with a mission to bring disciplined, professional parking management to the Austin, Texas market.

After years of service, Najee applied that same commitment to showing up, doing the job right, and never cutting corners to building a business the Austin community can rely on.

"The same discipline that made me effective in the military is what drives every decision at ParkTrooper. Your parking experience should be simple, secure, and dependable — every single time."

ParkTrooper LLC is a registered Texas LLC (SOS File No. 806558752), fully insured, and TDLR-compliant.
